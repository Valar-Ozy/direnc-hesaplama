HOW TO USE

  1. Enter the hourly production value for each building.
  2. Building 5 is optional. Leave it empty if you do not use it.
  3. Enter your target amount.
  4. Enter your current stock if available.
  5. The calculator will automatically show total hourly production, 24-hour production, remaining requirement, and required time.
  6. No data is stored, transmitted, or shared.
  7. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.
